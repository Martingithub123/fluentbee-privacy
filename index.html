Device Information — device type and OS version for compatibility purposes only.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit
  • Passwords stored as bcrypt hashes — never in plain text
  • Session tokens stored in iOS Keychain / Android Keystore
  • Row-level security ensuring users can only access their own data

6. Children's Privacy

FluentBee is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact us at support@fluentbee.app.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.
